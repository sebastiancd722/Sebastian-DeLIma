About Us

The Double Degree Club exists to support Laurier students pursuing both Business Administration and Computer Science. We create spaces where ambitious learners can collaborate, sharpen problem-solving skills, and explore careers that value interdisciplinary thinking. Through peer-led workshops, alumni mentorship, and partnerships with industry leaders, we help members gain confidence navigating co-op, case competitions, and technical interviews. Our community is passionate about building inclusive networks, sharing resources generously, and celebrating every win—big or small. Whether you are just starting first year or preparing for graduation, the club provides a launchpad to design the future you want and to surround yourself with people who will cheer you on every step of the way.

  • Peer mentorship and career panels each term
  • Hands-on sprints to level up case and coding skills
  • Inclusive community with socials that balance work and play
